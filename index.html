Who we are ?

Started as a home for destitute children to provide care and belongingness with 3 residents in 1999. We realised the power of life-centric education had to bring them to stability and vibrancy, hence the birth of Sumati Balwan. Now we have 650 students learning under a model of IBT (Introduction to Basic Technology) made available by the S.S.C board. We facilatate students to become emotionally intelligent and good human beings. With new-age skilled based education, they can help make India a developed nation and the world more eco-friendly. Focusing on entrepreneurship, creative expressions and an open classroom ‘build what you use’ model we want their bodies and minds to thrive.
